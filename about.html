How We Got Started

Decades of Logistics Excellence

Melons Logistics was formed in the 1990s to manage logistics operations for the Michael Cutler Company.

In 2011, we expanded to offer full 3PL (Third Party Logistics) services to clients beyond MCCO, marking a new chapter in our growth story.

Since then, we've grown into a trusted partner for transportation and freight management nationwide, serving diverse industries with precision and care.

Our team is built on professionalism, integrity, and open communication — values that ensure every shipment arrives safely and on time.
